Startup CTO

Let’s Go Give

CTO / Co-Founder · Aug 2020 – Feb 2022

Role focus

Built a bootstrapped product platform from the ground up.

I co-founded and built the platform for GoGive, a product for businesses to give charitable donations as gifts to employees, customers, and prospects.

Scope

As CTO/co-founder, I owned product and technical decisions across frontend, backend, local development, Kubernetes delivery, and onboarding workflows.

What I built

I developed frontend and backend applications, implemented Nix/direnv tooling for fast onboarding on Mac and Linux, and implemented Tilt for local development.

What I learned

Solo and small-team product development is different from operating inside a larger engineering organization. Every product, architecture, and tooling decision had to balance speed, maintainability, and bootstrapped constraints.

Why it mattered

The platform work made it possible to keep moving quickly without letting local development and deployment become a constant drag on product work.
