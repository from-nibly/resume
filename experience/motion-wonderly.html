Role focus

Made fast-moving engineering workflows safer and clearer.

I worked at the seams between product engineering and production systems: CI/CD, deploys, secrets, Kubernetes, incidents, and the developer workflows that make those systems usable.

A fast-moving engineering organization with large TypeScript and .NET monorepos, many delivery paths, and operational workflows that needed to become clearer, safer, and easier to repeat.

I built oncall-bot, an AI-assisted incident workflow for root-cause analysis and remediation. It helped turn 40 separate infrastructure issues into focused remediation PRs in one week.

Why it mattered

The common theme was removing friction so engineers could spend more time delivering product value and less time fighting builds, deploys, credentials, and production process.
